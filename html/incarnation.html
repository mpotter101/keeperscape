World Sprites - How To Use

Select an animation and then a facing.

Upload all frames for that animation in one-go (shift+click to select multiple images.

The amount of images you select will determine how many frames the current animation has.

All frames should have a 1:1 ratio. For best appearance, convert to 512x512 in your program of choice before upload.

When you have all of the animations and their facings filled out: scroll down, fill out your character's name, and save it to your profile. You can also export a json file for use in your own projects or to create an offline save of the project.

Be sure to scroll down and fill out your first-person sprites and character information
